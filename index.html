Features

Everything your ops
team actually needs.

01
🔍

Auto Field Discovery

CompliSync automatically discovers every custom field in your Jira instance and suggests intelligent mappings. No developer needed. Set up in under 10 minutes regardless of how your Jira is configured.

02
💬

Jira Comments Writeback

Edit the Jira Comments column in Sheets or Monday and CompliSync posts it back to Jira automatically — tagged with the source and user. The full comment thread stays in Jira where it belongs.

03
🔒

Private Notes Column

A dedicated Notes column that never touches Jira. Internal team context stays internal — preserved across every sync, never overwritten, never shared with clients.

04
⚡

Smart Conflict Resolution

Jira always wins on ticket data. For notes, you choose which platform is authoritative. No surprises, no lost edits, no manual reconciliation.

05
👤

External Reporter Detection

Automatically flags tickets submitted from outside your organization based on email domain. Instantly see which tickets came from clients vs. internal teams.

06
🔗

Linked Ticket Mapping

Captures up to three linked tickets per row — all clickable hyperlinks. See dependencies, duplicates, and related issues without ever leaving your sheet.

We never store ticket data on our servers. Your Jira API token is encrypted and stored in Google's ScriptProperties — not in any CompliSync database.
CompliSync provides the controls you need to handle PHI responsibly — field designation, PHI wipe, audit logging, data retention, and a BAA. But HIPAA compliance is your organization's responsibility, not ours. We give you the tooling; your team is responsible for configuring it correctly and following your own compliance policies. We strongly recommend involving your compliance officer before syncing any PHI.
Jira always wins on ticket data — status, priority, assignee, dates, and any Jira-owned fields. The one exception is the Notes column, which is user-owned and never overwritten by a sync. For the Jira Comments column, CompliSync detects whether a comment already exists in Jira before writing back, so you won't get duplicate entries.
Zapier is trigger-based — it fires when something changes, but it doesn't know the full state of your Jira board. CompliSync does a full bidirectional sync of every ticket matching your JQL filter, every time. It also lives inside Google Sheets as a sidebar, so there's no external tool to log into. And it has healthcare-specific features — PHI controls, external reporter detection, age flags — that generic automation tools simply don't offer.

Yes — unlimited Jira projects are included. Each project gets its own sheet tab and its own column configuration. You can have different column layouts, JQL filters, and Monday board IDs per project. Projects can sync to Sheets only, Monday only, or both simultaneously.
CompliSync sets up an automatic daily sync trigger that runs on weekdays. You can also hit "Sync Now" from the sidebar at any time for an on-demand sync. The sync schedule and frequency are configurable from the Settings tab in the sidebar.
When you click "Wipe PHI", CompliSync creates a copy of your sheet with all designated PHI columns replaced with [REDACTED]. Your original data is untouched — the wipe only affects the copy. You can then share the sanitized copy safely with external stakeholders, auditors, or anyone who doesn't need access to the underlying patient data. There's also an emergency wipe mode that clears PHI in-place from the live sheet, with a double-confirmation prompt.
