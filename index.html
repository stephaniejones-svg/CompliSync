Features

Everything your ops
team actually needs.

01
🔍

Auto Field Discovery

CompliSync automatically discovers every custom field in your Jira instance and suggests intelligent mappings. No developer needed. Set up in under 10 minutes regardless of how your Jira is configured.

02
💬

Jira Comments Writeback

Edit the Jira Comments column in Sheets or Monday and CompliSync posts it back to Jira automatically — tagged with the source and user. The full comment thread stays in Jira where it belongs.

03
🔒

Private Notes Column

A dedicated Notes column that never touches Jira. Internal team context stays internal — preserved across every sync, never overwritten, never shared with clients.

04
⚡

Smart Conflict Resolution

Jira always wins on ticket data. For notes, you choose which platform is authoritative. No surprises, no lost edits, no manual reconciliation.

05
👤

External Reporter Detection

Automatically flags tickets submitted from outside your organization based on email domain. Instantly see which tickets came from clients vs. internal teams.

06
🔗

Linked Ticket Mapping

Captures up to three linked tickets per row — all clickable hyperlinks. See dependencies, duplicates, and related issues without ever leaving your sheet.

Compliance Tier

Built for
healthcare teams.

The only Jira sync tool with a dedicated PHI compliance layer. Purpose-built for teams operating under HIPAA, HITRUST, or SOC 2.

🧹

One-Click PHI Wipe

Instantly sanitize designated PHI columns across all sheets. Generates a clean, share-safe version without touching your source data.

📅

Scheduled Sanitization

Set automatic PHI wipes on a schedule — daily, weekly, or monthly. Runs overnight so your team always starts the day compliant.

📋

Audit Log

Every sync, wipe, and share is logged with timestamp and user. Export your audit trail as a PDF for compliance reviews.

🗑️

Data Retention Policy

Automatically remove tickets older than a configurable threshold. Stay within your organization's data retention requirements without manual cleanup.

📄

HIPAA Documentation Export

Generate a compliance summary document showing your data flow, PHI handling procedures, and audit history — ready for your compliance officer.
